## What is Shelved?

**Shelved** is a web application that serves as a Letterboxd-esque clone for movies and books. It allows users to discover, save, and share their experiences with various media through its interactive platform. 

### Key Features:

- **Search for Media**: Easily search for movies and books by keyword or title.
- **Save Watched/Read Media**: Keep track of your media collection by adding them to your personal "shelf".
- **Post Reviews/Ratings**: Share your thoughts on movies or books by posting reviews and ratings.
- **Add Friends**: Connect with others by adding friends to your account.
- **Homepage Feed**: A dynamic feed showcasing trending media and updates.
- **See Friends' Likes/Reviews**: Stay updated on your friends' preferences and reviews.
- **User Authentication & Authorization**: Secure user login and account management for a personalized experience.
